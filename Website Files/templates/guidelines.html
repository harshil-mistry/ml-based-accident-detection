System Guidelines

Learn how to effectively use our AI-powered road safety features

How to Report an Accident

1

Access the Report Page

Click on the "Report" button in the navigation menu to access the accident reporting form.

2

Upload Media

Drag and drop or click to upload images/videos of the incident. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, MP4.

3

Provide Details

Fill in the location, time, and description of the incident. Be as specific as possible.

Understanding Smart Mapping

1

Map Navigation

Use the interactive map to view danger zones, traffic density, and reported incidents.

2

Color Codes

Green zones indicate safe areas, yellow for caution, and red for high-risk zones.

3

Route Planning

Use the suggested routes feature to find the safest path to your destination.

Safe Driving Guidelines

1

Pre-Drive Check

Check vehicle condition, mirrors, and ensure all safety systems are functioning properly before starting your journey.

2

During Drive

Maintain safe distance, follow speed limits, use turn signals, and stay focused on the road. Avoid using mobile phones while driving.

3

Emergency Response

If you encounter hazards, safely slow down, use hazard lights, and follow emergency protocols provided by our system.

Important Safety Guidelines

  • Always wear a seatbelt while driving
  • Avoid distractions like using mobile phones, eating, or adjusting the radio
  • Ensure your vehicle is in good condition before driving (check brakes, lights, tires, etc.)

  • Obey speed limits and road signs
  • Slow down in school zones, near hospitals, and crowded areas
  • Follow traffic signals and road markings

  • Walking Rules:
    • Always use sidewalks or footpaths
    • Cross roads at designated crosswalks
    • Look left, right, and left again before crossing
  • Awareness:
    • Avoid using mobile phones while crossing
    • Stay alert for reversing vehicles
    • Wear bright or reflective clothing at night

  • Wear a helmet and protective gear
  • Follow traffic rules as vehicles do
  • Use hand signals for turns or stops
  • Equip the cycle with lights and reflectors
  • Ride in designated cycling lanes when available
